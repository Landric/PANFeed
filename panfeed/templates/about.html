About the Project

What is PANFeed?

The purpose of PANFeed is to make academic news more accessable to all, whether you represent an individual, or an institution. We want tomake it easier to find the news you're interested in, and to simplify creating and publishing your own news feed.

Who are we?

We are a group of researchers based at the University of Southampton, in the Web And Internet Science research group. We created PANFeed as a part of the CampusROAR project, which is focused around making research more accessible, exciting and interesting, and to enable people to keep up to date on research in their areas of interest.


How can you contact us?
