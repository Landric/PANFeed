Welcome to PANFeed

PANFeed is a tool to help you take control of news feeds already existing on your university campus. It harvests feeds from university websites and uses them to build custom feeds of news you are actually interested in. The tool has a range of applications including personal custom news feeds, custom feeds for use in department websites or simply taking an inventory of your university feeds. Try out and take control of your news!

Find News

Use PANFeed to search for news directly related to your academic interests. Just enter some keywords and PANFeed will search it's vast store of academic news feeds to create your own personalised feed.

Read

Use PANFeed to create personalised news Digests - a collection of academic news feeds chosen by you, to read where you want, when you want.

Publish

Use PANFeed to create and publish Issues - resources that you want to share with the community, student projects, editorials and more!
