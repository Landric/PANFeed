Create an issue

A PANFeed issue is a completely custom feed. Each item is normal web link. Copy your links into the Add URLs box, Add these URLs as items and PANFeed try and guess a title description and image for each one. You have control over every single item. You can manually edit the title, description and change the image. You can even add editorial sections either by writing them in your blog or on Google Docs and linking them in or by using our custom "Editorial Item". This allows you put your personal spin on your feed and really tie the whole thing together.

Unlike a PANFeed digest your issue will need you to update it with new links over time but you have complete control of how and when those items are added.
